Raytracer

A Raytracer is a tool which renders realistic looking Computer Graphic Images.

This project was originally started during a course at RIT titled Global Illumination. The raytracer needs serious enhancements and improvements to compete with Pixar's RenderMan, but it was enlightening to construct nonetheless.

Somnus

A fiber is a lightweight thread of execution.

Somnus is a C++11 threading support library which provides a simple interface for writing programs with fibers and queues (which are known as channels).

While a fiber is waiting to enqueue or dequeue an element from a channel it is put to sleep. During this time other fibers are executed. The name was inspired by the Roman god of sleep, Somnus.
