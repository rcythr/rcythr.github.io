About

  • What is this?

    This is a small webapp that generates a random challenge note which the user is then expected to play on their MIDI-enabled instrument.

  • Why does it exist?

    For musical beginners the task of finding the right note to play when sight reading can be arduous. Playing music helps over time, but most songs do not span the whole range of an instrument.
